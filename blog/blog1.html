The Basics of Lean Manufacturing

Lean Manufacturing is a methodology focused on minimizing waste within manufacturing systems while simultaneously maximizing productivity. Key principles include:

  • Value Stream Mapping: Identify and optimize value-added activities.
  • Continuous Improvement (Kaizen): Foster a culture of constant process refinement.
  • Just-In-Time (JIT): Produce only what is needed when it is needed.

Implementing Lean Manufacturing can lead to reduced costs, improved quality, and faster delivery times, helping businesses stay competitive.
